### Root Cause Analysis

Classic IS/OOS regime mismatch. The IS (2020-2025) was dominated by a multi-year crypto
bull cycle where EMA crossover + RSI momentum is structurally rewarded. The OOS
(2025-2026) is a choppy, directionally ambiguous regime with BTC down -14% on the year.

In choppy regimes:
- EMAs oscillate around each other → frequent false crossovers
- RSI 50 crossings happen on noise, not trend initiation
- ADX > 25 gate is not sufficient to filter ranging markets in this regime

The simultaneous dual-crossover requirement (EMA AND RSI on same bar) makes entries very
sparse (n=15 OOS) and late — by the time both fire, the initial move is often complete.

The original HLHB was designed for forex trend trading. Forex has smoother autocorrelation
and lower volatility regimes. Crypto 4H has fat-tailed volatility spikes that trigger
false crossovers which then immediately reverse.
