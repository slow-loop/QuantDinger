## 第一輪搜尋：傳統技術分析社群策略

### 可 port 列表（OHLC-native，無需 alt data）

**1. ICT Fair Value Gap (FVG) + Liquidity Sweep**
- Source: TradingView ICT community, Bitget Silver Bullet guide
- Hypothesis: Price sweeps liquidity pool (fake breakout above/below N-bar high/low), leaves FVG
  (3-candle gap), institutional re-entry in the gap. Entry at FVG midpoint after CHoCH.
- Entry: liquidity_sweep (bar high > prev N-bar max, closes back below) + CHoCH + price enters FVG
- Exit: prior swing high (TP) / FVG bottom (SL)
- Pattern type: structural catalyst (liquidity grab → FVG fill)
- TV file: `tv_fvg_liquidity_sweep_eth_4h_long.py`

**2. NR7 Volatility Compression → Breakout + EMA Gate**
- Source: TradingView NR4/NR7 scripts, LuxAlgo NR4/NR7 with Breakouts
- Hypothesis: Bar with narrowest range in 7 bars = extreme compression → expansion follows.
  Enter breakout direction only when aligned with trend (price > EMA50).
- Entry: bar range = min(high-low, last 7 bars) + close > NR7 bar high + close > EMA50
- Exit: ATR trail or fixed TP / NR7 bar low = SL
- TV file: `tv_nr7_compression_breakout_eth_4h_long.py`

**3. Bar Counter Exhaustion + S/R Gate**
- Source: TradingView "Bar Counter Trend Reversal" (569 likes)
- Hypothesis: N consecutive bearish bars near a structural support = momentum exhaustion.
  First bullish bar after exhaustion = entry.
- Entry: 5+ consecutive close < open bars + price near swing low (within 2×ATR) + first close > open
- Exit: prior swing high / timeout
- TV file: `tv_bar_exhaustion_reversal_eth_4h_long.py`

**4. VWAP Reclaim Momentum (≠ reversion)**
- Source: Snappchart VWAP Momentum Strategy
- CRITICAL NOTE: tv_vwap_stretch_reversion (archived) entered when price was STRETCHED from VWAP.
  This strategy enters when price RECLAIMS VWAP from below with volume spike — opposite logic.
- Hypothesis: Price consolidates below VWAP then crosses above it with 2× volume = average-cost
  buyers flipped from losers to winners; momentum follows.
- Entry: close > VWAP[rolling 50] AND close[-1] < VWAP[-1] AND volume > 2× vol_SMA20
- Exit: VWAP break below (stop) or prior high (TP)
- TV file: `tv_vwap_reclaim_momentum_eth_4h_long.py`

**5. Doji + Demand Zone + EMA200 Trend Filter**
- Source: TradingView "Twisted Forex's Doji+Area" (217 likes)
- Hypothesis: Doji (indecision candle) inside a demand zone + EMA200 trend confirmation = 
  institutional accumulation visible; resolve long.
- Entry: |close-open|/(high-low) < 0.25 + price in demand_zone (near swing low ± ATR) + close > EMA200
- Exit: supply zone above (TP) / zone invalidation (SL)
- TV file: `tv_doji_demand_zone_eth_4h_long.py`

**6. Liquidation Wick Sweep Proxy**
- Source: CoinGlass liquidation heatmap analysis, Glassnode liquidation research
- Hypothesis: Large lower wick sweeping multi-bar low = leverage longs liquidated (forced market
  sell) and immediately absorbed. Price snaps back = longs cleared, sellers exhausted.
- Entry: wick_ratio = (close-low)/(high-low) > 0.7 + low < N-bar rolling low + close > prev close
- Exit: ATR trail / timeout
- TV file: `tv_liq_wick_sweep_eth_4h_long.py`

**7. Extended Pump Fade (CEX Listing proxy)**
- Source: CEX listing studies (98% Binance-listed tokens dump, avg 52% post-peak decline)
- Hypothesis: Any asset that pumps >30% in 5 bars then shows reversal signals is statistically
  likely to mean-revert. Models the CEX listing post-peak fade WITHOUT needing external event data.
- Entry SHORT: 5-day gain > 30% + RSI > 70 + first bearish engulfing or close below EMA9
- Exit: -15% from peak or 10-bar timeout
- TV file: `tv_pump_fade_eth_4h.py` (short direction)
- Note: QuantDinger tradeDirection = short; need to check if platform supports shorts

**8. BTC.D Proxy Regime Gate (ETH/BTC relative strength)**
- Source: BTC Dominance analysis (BTC.D < 59.63% = altseason), DEXTools BTC.D guide
- Implementation: Use ETH vs its own 200-bar EMA + BTC price relative trend as proxy
  (since we don't have BTC.D as a column in standard OHLC)
- Hypothesis: When ETH outperforms (price > EMA200 AND EMA50 > EMA200), altcoin season dynamics
  are in play. Gate all existing alt strategies on this condition.
- TV file: `tv_btcd_proxy_regime_eth_4h_long.py`

## 事後分析：為什麼 8 個策略全部 OOS 失敗？

### 根本原因：IS/OOS 制度差異

IS 期間（2020–2025）包含 2020–2021 大牛市 + 2023–2024 牛市。
OOS 期間（2025–2026）為熊市 + 橫盤：ETH -40%，BTC -25%。

所有使用 EMA 趨勢過濾（EMA50 > EMA200、close > EMA200）的策略在 OOS 幾乎無法入場，
因為熊市下 EMA gate 永久關閉。這種「只做多頭趨勢」的設計在牛市看起來像 alpha，
實際是 regime bias。

### 個別失敗原因分類

| 失敗類型 | 策略 | 說明 |
|---|---|---|
| EMA gate 過濾熊市 | NR7、Doji、BTC.D proxy | IS gate 選擇牛市入場，OOS 無信號或信號質量暴降 |
| 機制本身無 edge | VWAP reclaim | IS 也是 -1.420，根本不是套利機制 |
| IS/OOS 制度差異 | Bar exhaustion | 連跌後反彈在熊市繼續跌；S/R gate 無效 |
| 過度入場（overfire） | FVG liq sweep | 153 IS trades = 55% TiM，幾乎無時不在場 |
| 0 trades（條件過嚴） | Pump fade | 大型市場罕見 25%+漲幅 + 20%跌幅 + EMA反彈同時發生 |

### 唯一例外：tv_liq_wick_sweep_eth_4h_long

IS IR = 2.102（elite grade）說明 IS 信號品質真實，不僅是制度 bias。
清算瀑布機制（強制平倉 → 吸收 → 反彈）在牛熊市都存在，只是熊市回彈幅度較小。
OOS 樣本僅 18 筆（15 個月），統計意義有限。保留 active 狀態待 BTC/SOL 跨資產驗證。
