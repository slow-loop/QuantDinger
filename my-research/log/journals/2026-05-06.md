### 1. Factor validation — htf_reclaim_retest ❌ ARCHIVED

(reclaim → failure). The slow 140-bar MA makes the signal lag the regime shift by months.

### 2. Factor validation — horizontal_reclaim ⚠️ ACTIVE (watching)
Ran event tester on `factor_horizontal_reclaim.py` (BTC/USDT 1D):
- IS 30d: hit 67.5% n=255, avg +10.74%, Sharpe +0.57 — strong IS edge
- OOS 30d: hit 54.5% n=11, avg -6.49%, Sharpe -0.53 — hit rate holds but avg return inverted

The hit rate staying above 50% OOS is encouraging (the factor identifies bullish reclaims
more often than not), but the magnitude of losses exceeds wins in the 2025-26 period. This
is a choppy-market artifact: BTC saw repeated false breakouts above the 50d high that failed
at 1-7 day horizons before recovering at 30d.

Decision: Keep active. IS evidence is robust (n=255, not a lucky sample). OOS n=11 is thin.
Do not use in strategy until OOS expected value turns positive — revisit in 3-6 months.
